Technology

Built on Gun.js, a decentralized peer-to-peer database. Data syncs across nodes without central servers.

Encryption

All data encrypted with SEA (Security, Encryption, Authorization). Only you can decrypt your notes.

Sync Issues

If notes dont sync between devices, ensure at least one relay is connected on both devices. Try adding the same custom relay on both.

Self-Host

For reliable sync, run your own relay: npx gun-server. Then add your URL as custom relay.
